The Pauli-X gate is similar to the classical NOT gate, flipping the $|0\rangle$ state to $|1\rangle$ and vice versa.

## Matrix

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
  0 & 1 \\\\
  1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
$$

### More Details

This gate is also useful for generating entanglement when combined with other gates.
