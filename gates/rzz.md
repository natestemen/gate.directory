The $R_{zz}$ gate implements an Ising-type interaction with rotation angle $\theta$.

$$
\begin{align*}
R_{zz}(\theta) & = \exp\left(-i\frac{\theta}{2}Z\otimes Z\right) \\\\
  & =
\begin{bmatrix}
    \mathrm{e}^{-i\frac{\theta}{2}} & 0                     & 0                     & 0 \\\\
    0                      & \mathrm{e}^{i\frac{\theta}{2}} & 0                     & 0 \\\\
    0                      & 0                     & \mathrm{e}^{i\frac{\theta}{2}} & 0 \\\\
    0                      & 0                     & 0                     & \mathrm{e}^{-i\frac{\theta}{2}}
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
$$

### Properties

- Entangling for $\theta$ not a multiple of $\pi$.
- Can be implemented with two CNOTs and a single-qubit $R_z$ rotation.
