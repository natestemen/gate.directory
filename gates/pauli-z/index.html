Pauli-Z Gate

Symbol

$Z$

Description:

The Pauli-Z gate is known as a phase flip, leaving $|0\rangle$ unchanged and sending $|1\rangle \to -|1\rangle$.
Notation(s) $Z$, $\sigma_z$, $\sigma_3$
Matrix $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{bmatrix}$

Group $\mathsf{U}(2)$
