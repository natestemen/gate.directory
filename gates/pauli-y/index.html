Pauli-Y Gate

Symbol

$Y$

Description:

The Pauli-Y gate is similar to the classical NOT gate, but introduces an imaginary $i$ sending $|0\rangle \to i|1\rangle$ and $|1\rangle \to -i|0\rangle$.
Notation(s) $Y$, $\sigma_y$, $\sigma_2$
Matrix $\begin{bmatrix}0 & -i \\ i & 0 \end{bmatrix}$
Arity 1
Group $\mathsf{U}(2)$

Groups

  • clifford
