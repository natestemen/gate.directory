R_y(\theta) = \exp\left(-i\frac{\theta}{2}Y\right) =
\begin{bmatrix}
    \phantom{+}\cos\frac{\theta}{2}    & -\sin\frac{\theta}{2} \\\\

\end{bmatrix}
